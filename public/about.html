About FinFriend

Empowering East African students with the financial knowledge they need to thrive.

Our Mission

FinFriend was created to address a critical gap: most Ugandan university students graduate without basic financial literacy skills. We believe every student deserves access to practical, engaging, and culturally relevant financial education.

The Problem

Financial illiteracy leads to:

  • Poor budgeting and overspending during university
  • Falling victim to predatory loan apps
  • Inability to save or invest for the future
  • Financial stress that affects academic performance

Our Solution

FinFriend provides:

  • Interactive Modules – Bite-sized lessons with quizzes on budgeting, saving, investing, and credit management.
  • Personalized Dashboard – Track your expenses and savings goals in one place.
  • Financial Calculators – Budget planner, investment return calculator, and loan repayment tool — all using UGX.
  • Community Forum – Connect with peers, ask questions, and share tips.
  • Expert Insights – Blog posts and advice from financial professionals in East Africa.
  • Gamification – Earn XP, badges, and climb the leaderboard as you learn.

Target Audience

University students (undergraduate and postgraduate) in Uganda, expanding to the broader East African region including Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

Our Team

FinFriend is built by passionate university students and graduates who experienced firsthand the challenges of managing money during their studies. We're committed to making financial education accessible, engaging, and impactful.
